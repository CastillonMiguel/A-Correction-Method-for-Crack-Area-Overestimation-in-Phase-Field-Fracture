Overstimation
-------------
Here the overestimation of the crack surface area in phase-field fracture simulations is analyzed. The scripts in this directory generate plots for the analytical solutions of a one-dimensional bar problem, illustrating the phase-field profile (:math:`\phi(x)`), its derivative (:math:`\phi'(x)`), and the energy of the bar with the strain localization effect. The overestimation is quantified by comparing the computed crack area with the analytical solution, highlighting the discrepancies that arise due to numerical artifacts and mesh resolution.
